Our Principal

Madam Anne Awino is the Principal Lanet Secondary School

The Principal's Message

"We are looking forward to a productive partnership with you to ensure our children can achieve their highest potential. We recognize that to be successful in school, our children need support from both home and school. We know a strong partnership with you will make a significant difference in your child's education."
